# Nialli™ Visual Planner: Phase Planning Research

## The Phase Pull View

- **Goal**: Aligns teams by working backward from a target milestone to identify the necessary sequence and duration of tasks.
- **Lanes**: Must be created in the Weekly Planning view first.

## Master Activities

- **Importing**: Import view-only activities from external schedules (P6, MS Project).
- **Conversion**: Drag a master activity into a phase pull lane to create an editable phase tag. Each derived tag remains linked to its master activity ID.

## Stage Area (Phase)

- A collapsible workspace to prepare activities before placing them on the timeline, ideal for pre-populating work before a collaborative session.

## Longest Path

- **Critical Flow**: Identifies the chain of dependent activities that takes the most time to reach a milestone.
- **Visibility**: Highlighted with blue connector lines on the board to focus team discussions on critical tasks.

## Phase to Week View

- A split-screen interface (Phase Pull on top, Weekly Planning on bottom).
- **Transition**: Drag phase activities into the weekly view to break them down into detailed daily tasks.
- **Highlighting**: Selecting a phase activity highlights all its corresponding daily tags in the weekly view.
